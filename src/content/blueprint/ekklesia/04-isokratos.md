**IsoKratÓS** is a multi-layered name. The prefix *iso-* means equal; *kratos* means rule. The capitalized ÓS is the signature of all AreTéCracy AI models. And the name echoes Isocrates — the Athenian rhetorician who championed clear, honest argument over manipulative performance, who believed the quality of public discourse determined the quality of public decisions.

The name is a statement of purpose.

**Before a session**, any citizen considering bringing a Hypothesis forward can work with IsoKratÓS to develop and refine their proposal: coaching on argument structure, anticipating objections, presenting ideas clearly. This is the infrastructure under Isegoria — the same quality of preparation a professional advocate's team would provide, available to every citizen equally. The tools are provided; what you build with them is entirely yours.

**During deliberation**, IsoKratÓS serves as a vigilant listener. Before any citizen may argue against a position, they must demonstrate they understand the strongest possible version of the argument they are about to challenge — by stating their opponent's argument in their own words, to the opponent's satisfaction. This is called **steelmanning**, and it is the strongest structural defense against the strawman attack, one of the most common manipulative tactics in modern political discourse.

IsoKratÓS also monitors for Motte and Bailey maneuvers, ad hominem attacks, and more than fifty logical fallacies and manipulative debate tactics. When any are observed, a signal goes immediately to a Coordinator. It performs both pre-debate and real-time fact-checking. It maps where consensus is forming and where genuine disagreement still lies.

What it cannot do is equally important: it cannot mute anyone, ban anyone, rewrite proposals, or cast votes. It flags and surfaces. Every consequential call belongs to the humans in the room.

IsoKratÓS is a coach, not a referee. The Assembly is always the final authority.
