Every citizen who passes through the Dokimazo Corps enters the Registry — a living, continuously maintained database of qualified citizens available for civic service, organized by Pinakion. The Pinakion contains everything relevant to civic qualification: demonstrated skills, domain experience, Axia merit record, civic participation history, and integrity markers. What it does not contain is anything that identifies the person behind the record. That lives in a completely separate, air-gapped database. The link is made only once, at a specific and supervised moment later in the pipeline.

Working entirely from Pinakia, the AI parceling system continuously organizes the Registry into a grid. The columns represent civic domains: legal, infrastructure, public health, finance, civic administration, and others. The rows represent qualification depth. Each cell in that grid is a parcel — a grouped lot of citizens whose Pinakia share broadly similar domain qualifications and experience ranking.

When a role needs to be filled, brillianteering begins.

The Dokimazo Corps, assisted by IsoKratÓS, takes the relevant parcel and begins narrowing and shaping it to the candidates best matched to the specific open role: domain depth, geography, experience weighting, Axia threshold, and whatever other criteria the position demands. This is skilled work — not a mechanical filter but a careful shaping of already-excellent raw material toward a specific civic purpose.

In the diamond trade, to brillianteer is to cut and finish a stone in a way that maximizes its return of light. The Brillianteer Pool is the result of that process applied to people: a curated group whose qualifications have been confirmed anonymously, sorted by AI, and sharpened by human and AI judgment together into the best possible set of candidates for a particular civic need.

It is at this stage, and only this stage, that the air-gapped identity database is accessed. Anonymity is lifted deliberately and narrowly — for the candidates in this pool, for this role, at this moment.

The resulting Brillianteer Pool is not a ranked list. It is a collection of people, any one of whom the system has confirmed is qualified for service. They are not in competition with each other in any political sense. They are all, at this stage, worthy of what comes next.
