##### "Protectors of the Hypothesis"

*From Greek: aigis (αἰγίς, shield/protection)*

A trained civil service body that helps refine citizen proposals into legally precise Psephismata, or bills to be voted on.  This will include attorneys and paralegals, along with  experts in the domain that the given Pnyx serves, such as doctors, engineers, educators, etc.

The DikaiOS AI provides Aegides with oversight and assistance to preserve Ekklesia intent, compliance with precedent, and more.

**Function:** Bring the stated intent of the Ekklesia—"what citizens want"—into legally enforceable language that adheres stringently to their will and intent.

**Selection:** Members are selected through:

* Dokimazo (vetting or "tempering"), which includes education, degrees, certifications, work history, potential skills or competency testing, interviews, etc.

* Axia (citizenship-wide scoring system for civic merit and honorable reputation)

* Approval of a candidate list by the Ekklesia

* Sortition (random lot) from the candidate list.

This is intended to ensure against political factioning while at the same time ensuring ability, skill, and integrity.
