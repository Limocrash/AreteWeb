##### *Founding Document / Constitution / Magna Carta*

*From Greek: archē (ἀρχή, beginning/foundation) + gramma (γράμμα, writing/document)*

The foundational constitutional document encoding Isotimía and the Four Isótēs into enforceable law. Both the principles themselves, as well as hard constraints and enforcement mechanisms, are established in this document.

**Key features:**

- Encodes Isotimía as a constitutional mandate
- Defines Axioktonia (self-destruct clause for regime failure that reboots government)
- Requires extreme Homonoia for amendments
- Includes "Horizon Clauses" for foreseeable future challenges that do not yet directly affect peoples' lives, but which clearly will in time.

**See also:** Axioktonia, Homonoia, Horizon Clauses
