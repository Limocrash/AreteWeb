#### *A totally refactored OS for Democracy (replaces versions 2.XXX and restores virtue from version 1.0)*

*From Greek: aretē (ἀρετή, excellence/virtue) + kratia (κρατία, rule/power)*

A civic governance operating system built on virtue (aretē), equal standing (Isotimía), and direct citizen participation. Combines ancient Athenian democratic principles with modern AI tools to restore genuine self-governance.
