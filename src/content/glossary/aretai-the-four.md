##### *The Four Virtues*

*From Greek: aretē (ἀρετή, virtue/excellence)*

The civic virtues that form the "geological composition" of Isotimía's bedrock. Not aspirational ideals but foundational requirements for any intelligence (human or otherwise) to share a world without catastrophe.

1. **PRC (Plato's Roll Call)** - Déon (δέον, binding duty) + Ergon (ἔργον, civic work): The obligation to participate in self-governance
2. **Philotimo** (Φιλότιμο) - Honor and duty to others: Using your standing for common good, not just personal gain
3. **Sonder** - Recognition of others' full humanity: Everyone else is as real and complex as you are
4. **Alētheia** (Ἀλήθεια) - Truth and transparency: Commitment to reality over comfortable fictions

**See also:** Isotimía, PRC, Philotimo, Sonder, Alētheia
