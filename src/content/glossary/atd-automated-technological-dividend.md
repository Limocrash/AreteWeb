ATD is one of the Horizon Clauses. This one addresses automation displacing human labor. When productivity rises due to AI and robotics and causes jobs to disappear, ATD ensures value flows to citizens, not just capital owners.

ATD does not constitute passive payments as in UBI (Universal Basic Income). Instead, it offers access to and ownership of the productive capacity (tools, training, infrastructure) that is replacing those jobs, and requires active participation in the polis.

**ATD is implemented through flexible combinations of:**

- Open-source AI and robotics
- Workshop Economy infrastructure
- PAIA (tools and training, not just cash)
- Public ownership of critical automation

**Phase:** Near-term (10-20 years) response to displacement
**Evolution:** Path to post-scarcity economics

**See also:** Hephaistia, Workshop Economy, PAIA, Horizon Clauses
