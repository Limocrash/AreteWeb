##### *Self-Destruct Clause For Dissolution of Government*

*From Greek: axios (ἄξιος, worthy) + kteinein (κτείνειν, to kill) = "worthy death"*

The self-destruct clause in the Archegramma. Defines when the regime has failed so fundamentally that it must be legally dissolved and rebooted. Jokingly referred to as the 'Jim Phelps Clause' by α-Team members.

**Triggers might include:**

- Systematic violation of Isotimía
- Attempts at factional seizure of any institution or policy
- Erosion of or direct damage to the Four Isótēs
- Failure of accountability systems (Logodosia)

**Process:** A special Archegramma Dicesis assembly requiring ***Pampollē Homonoia*** (an oversized supermajority) and judicial agreement to declare failure and transition to new constitutional convention.

**See also:** Archegramma, Homonoia, Isotimía
