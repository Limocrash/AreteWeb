Principle that policies must be fair across time and generations, not just across current voters. Prevents current generation from looting the future.

**Applications:**

- Debt and deficit limits
- Climate and resource policy
- Long-term infrastructure investment
- Intergenerational transfer accounting

**Implemented through:** Generational Fairness Fund, Paideia, and more.

**See also:** GFF, Oikonomia, IsoEudaimonia
