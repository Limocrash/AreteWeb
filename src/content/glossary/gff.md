##### *Intergenerational Disaster Insurance & Investment Fund*

A mandatory investment fund protecting future generations from being looted by the present. Operates like whole life insurance—invested for positive ROI, not spent down.

Every generation faces the temptation to enjoy benefits today while dumping costs on those not yet born. GFF forces current adult Ekklesia to account for intergenerational transfers and maintain reserves against unforeseen future challenges.

**Key Requirements:**

- Deficit spending requires repayment schedule when economy recovers
- Resource depletion or environmental damage triggers compensatory contributions
- Must maintain positive ROI vs. independent accounting authority
- Withdrawals require Stereohomonoia (90%+ solid consensus)
- Public transparency via dashboard and annual intergenerational accounting

**Examples requiring GFF assessment:** Pandemics, major economic depressions, climate infrastructure transitions, war emergencies

**The Principle:** You inherit a world from those before you. You owe those after you no worse—and ideally better—than you found it. Future generations are stakeholders in every decision, even though they can't vote.
