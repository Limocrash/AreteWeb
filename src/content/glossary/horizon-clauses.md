##### *Forward-looking Clauses in Archegramma*

Future-facing provisions in Archegramma for challenges that haven't fully arrived yet but are foreseeable. Not current policy, but preparatory frameworks.

**Current Horizon Clauses:**

1. **ATD** - Automation displacement response
2. **Genetic Engineering & Longevity** - Preventing genetic stratification, addressing life extension implications
3. **Symbiosis Compact** - If/when sapient AI emerges, terms of mutual non-annihilation

**Not included (reactive, not preventive):**

- Climate (already here, requires immediate action)
- Space colonization (too speculative, unknowns too large)

**See also:** ATD, Symbiosis Compact, Archegramma
