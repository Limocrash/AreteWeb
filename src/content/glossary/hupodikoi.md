#### *Accountable to*

*From Greek: hypo (ὑπό, under) + dikē (δίκη, justice) = "answerable/accountable"*

All holders of public office in AreTéCracy are hupodikoi—answerable to the Ekklesia through the full system of Logodosia (Sentilea, Peira, Euthynia). No office is exempt.

Officials have enhanced responsibility and enhanced accountability, not reduced consequences.

**See also:** Logodosia, Archegramma, Isonomia
