##### *Initial Proposed Bill from Ekklesia*

*From Greek: hypo (ὑπό, under) + thesis (θέσις, placing) = "foundation/supposition"*

A citizen's initial proposal for law or policy. The starting point of Nomothetēsis. Can come from any citizen, but must pass through:

- Clarity check (is it comprehensible?)
- Feasibility review (is it possible?)
- Archegramma compliance (does it violate core principles?)

**See also:** Nomothetēsis, Psephisma, Dokimazo
