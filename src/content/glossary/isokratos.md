##### *AreTéCracy's AI for Assisting in Civic Delibiration*

*From Greek: isos (ἴσος, equal) + kratos (κράτος, power) + Operating System*

The civic deliberation operating system. AI-powered platform enabling millions to deliberate without descending into chaos or capture.

**Functions:**

- Structures debates (prevents thread chaos)
- Oratory assistance (helps citizens communicate clearly)
- Axia tracking (domain-specific merit/reputation)
- Decorum enforcement (Sonder, Philotimo, Alētheia)
- Session moderation (for both oral and commentary threads)
- Explainable AI (Alētheia requirement - no black boxes)

**See also:** DikaiOS, Ekklesia, Pnyx, Axia
