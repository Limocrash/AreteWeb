##### *Equal citizenship*

*From Greek: isos (ἴσος, equal) + politeia (πολιτεία, citizenship)*

One of the Four Isótēs. Same rights, duties, and civic access for all citizens. Citizenship is reciprocal obligation, not passive status.

**Key concepts:**

- Sortition (selection by lot for civic roles)
- Dokimazo (vetting for competence, not wealth)
- Universal civic education (Paideia)
- Equal access to governance roles (no permanent political class)

**See also:** The Four Isótēs, Sortition, Dokimazo, Paideia
