##### *Accountability / Transparency*

*From Byzantine/Modern Greek: logos (λόγος, account/reckoning) + didomi (δίδωμι, to give)*

The accountability stack. **The goal of Logodosia is transparency and Alētheia**—ensuring those who hold power operate in the light, not in shadow.

All public servants are hupodikoi (ὑπόδικοι, answerable) to the Ekklesia. Power comes with enhanced responsibility and enhanced scrutiny, not immunity.

**Four accountability layers:**

**Layer 1 - Glaukós Mati (Reverse Surveillance):** Cameras pointed UP at government, not down at citizens. Creates tamper-evident records of official conduct. Designed to create complete transparency and prevent corruption by monitoring public offices

**Layer 2 - Sentilea** (Συντέλεια): Regular in-term reporting to Ekklesia, verified against surveillance records

**Layer 3 - Peira** (Πεῖρα): Triggered investigations when problems arise, using evidence from surveillance and reports

**Layer 4 - Euthynia** (Εὐθυνία): End-of-term comprehensive audit drawing on all previous layers

Officials who serve with integrity have nothing to fear from transparency. Those who abuse power have nowhere to hide.
