##### *Corrupted Professional Politician*

*From Greek myth: half-human, half-bull creature that demanded human sacrifice*

In AreTéCracy: a public servant who serves two masters—the people (to get reelected) and the pleonektai (to get funded). Metaphorically neither fully human citizen nor fully captured beast, the minotaur represents the corrupted professional politician who can no longer serve either role honestly.

Modern example: Politicians who must serve donors to afford campaigns, while pretending to serve constituents.

**See also:** Pleonektai, Logodosia, Isonomia
