##### ***Ostracism*** / *"Removal of Concentrated Power"*

*From Greek: ostrakon (ὄστρακον, potsherd/shard used for voting)*

In classical Athens, citizens could vote once a year to exile someone for ten years without trial—not as punishment for crimes, but to *defuse dangerously concentrated power* before it could tear the polis apart.

In AreTéCracy, Ostrakismos is a tightly constrained constitutional emergency tool aimed *squarely*at oligarchic power—whether held by **private pleonektai** or **public office-holders (Minotaurs)** using their position to build or serve oligarchy. It exists when normal checks (Logodosia, Axia decay, conflict-of-interest rules, recall) are no longer enough to protect Isotimía.

**For private pleonektai:** Civic and economic quarantine for a defined period (typically 10 years)—forced divestiture from Survival Stack sectors, ownership caps, exclusion from control roles, limits on political activity.

**For office-holders (Minotaurs):** Reserved for autocratic behavior beyond ordinary misconduct—using state power to enrich oneself, shield from accountability, or entrench personal rule. Functions as direct constitutional impeachment: removal from office and disqualification *by the people.* Last resort instead of Axioktonia (dissolution of government).

Specific enforcement mechanisms detailed in Tier 3.

Use requires high Homonoia threshold, independent review, and clear objective criteria—not partisan rage. Last-resort firewall against democratic failure, not shortcut around due process.
