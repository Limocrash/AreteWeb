##### *Investigative Hearing of an Official*

*From Greek: peira (πεῖρα, trial/attempt/experience)*

Third layer of Logodosia accountability.

Triggered investigation by Ekklesia when something goes wrong. Launched by:

- Whistleblowers
- Anomalies in surveillance logs
- Ekklesia concern
- Failed Sentilea metrics

**See also:** Logodosia, Sentilea, Euthynia, Reverse Surveillance
