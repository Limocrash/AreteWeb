##### *Love of Honor*

*From philo– (φίλο-, love/affection) + timē (τιμή, honor, worth).*

One of the Four Aretai. Philotimo is one of those philosophical Greek words with no English equivalent. It is "love of honor," but without ego. It’s the felt obligation to do what is right and to help others—even when no one is watching nor reward expected—and the joy of knowing you have not failed your own conscience or your community.

In AreTéCracy, Philotimo and Sonder are the Aretai that refuse to treat other people as NPCs or insignificant. It shows up as returning more than you take, stepping up when something needs doing, admitting mistakes without being forced, and not seeking unfair advantage .

**See also:** Isotimía, Sonder, Alētheia, Pleonektai, Logodosia.
