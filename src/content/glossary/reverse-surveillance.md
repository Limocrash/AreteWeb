First layer of Logodosia accountability.

Cameras and monitoring pointed UP at government, not down at citizens. Creates tamper-evident records of official conduct. Privacy limits decided by Ekklesia, with compensation for scrutiny.

**Principle:** Power should be transparent; anyone serving the people should be *more* accountable to upholding Isotímia than the people. Therefore, they should accept *less* privacy and more transparency than the people.

**Partial List of Tools:**

- Public logs of official actions
- Recordings of deliberations (except legitimate security/privacy needs)
- Real-time dashboards of government activity
- Citizen access to oversight data

**See also:** Logodosia, Asphaleia, Alētheia
