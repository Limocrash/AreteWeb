##### *Regular Reporting to Ekklesia*

*From Greek: syn (σύν, together) + telos (τέλος, completion/tax) = "joint contribution"*

Second layer of Logodosia accountability.

Regular in-term reporting by every magistrate-level Therapon to Ekklesia.

Includes:

- Monthly/quarterly performance reports
- Requests for Ekklesia approval on major decisions
- Budget transparency
- Early warning of problems

**See also:** Logodosia, Peira, Euthynia
