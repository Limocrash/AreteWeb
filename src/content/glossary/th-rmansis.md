##### *"Tempering / Refinement"*

_From Greek: thermainō (θερμαίνω, to heat/temper)_

The vetting and refinement process where citizen proposals (Hypothesis) are tempered into legally precise, enforceable laws (Psephismata). Aegides work with DikaiOS to test the proposal for Archegramma compliance, identify loopholes, clarify ambiguities, and preserve the Ekklesia's intent while ensuring the law actually does what citizens want it to do.

Like tempering clay tablets—heating and shaping to remove weaknesses and create durability—Thérmansis refines raw ideas into workable policy without changing the fundamental intent.
