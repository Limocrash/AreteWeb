headline: "POLITICIANS CAN'T BE BOUGHT. (THERE AREN'T ANY.)"
---

In AreTéCracy, there are no rulers; only citizens. All officials are functionaries, not commanders. They can't do anything the people don't choose to allow. There are no career politicians, no dynasties, and there's no way to inherit or buy an office, even if it mattered — which it doesn't.

For high-level offices, citizens are selected from a refined list of well-qualified individuals from the populace at large, completely without favor. What counts are proven merit as citizens, verified skills, and legitimate knowledge. Pedigrees do not; neither wealth nor status nor the name of one's university; and they cannot influence the process. Once the list is approved by an overwhelming number of the people, the final choice is up to the Fates, without politics or favor. Competence is guaranteed without politics, donors or insincere promises.
