In AreTéCracy, there are no rulers; only citizens. All officials are functionaries, not commanders. They can’t do anything the people don’t choose to allow. There are no career politicians, no dynasties, and no way to inherit or buy any office, even if that office mattered — which it doesn’t.

For high-level offices, citizens are chosen from a refined list of well-qualified individuals out of the populace at large, completely without political favor or bias. What counts are proven merit as citizens, verified skills, and legitimate knowledge. Pedigrees do not. Neither wealth, nor status, nor the name of one’s university can influence the process. Once the list is approved by an overwhelming number of the people, the final choice is up to the Fates, again without political favor or bias. Competence is guaranteed without politics, donors or insincere promises.

To understand more about how offices are filled in an AreTéCracy, see *The Peoples Government* in the Blueprint section.
