Are you already a suspected thief or criminal? If not, then why are the cameras pointed at *you* and not the ones watching you when we already don't trust *them*? Let's turn the cameras around. That's what GlaukÓS Mati, AreTéCracy's "Reverse Surveillance" security system does.

Accountability and transparency are critical elements of AreTéCracy. But they do not come from "checks and balances" or "separation of powers" where people we have little reason to trust watch over other people we have little reason to trust. All transparency flows directly to the people, and the people decide how to respond.

Like the Glaux, the owl of Athens emblazoned opposite Athena herself on Athens' preeminent coin, GlaukÓS Mati is a wise and untiring guardian who is vigilant even in the dark. Every action, transaction, and decision made by a public servant is recorded on an immutable public ledger. There are no backroom deals because there are no backrooms, no lair for secrecy. It's simple: If you serve the public, you live in a glass house.
