headline: "FORGET UBI. THERE'S ONE JOB AI CAN NEVER TAKE AWAY: YOURS."
---

While we understand the arguments that have been put forward, from different sides, about the topic of what we do when the advancement of AI and robotics make nearly all of the current job market obsolete (except for a very specific few), we nevertheless hesitate. That is because we honestly think every one of those sides is forgetting to count the one job we gave away over 2,000 years ago, and that no robot can ever do in a human's stead. That job is self-governance.

Once we reclaim that right instead of outsourcing it, we see an entirely different future when mankind has developed the tools to have technology do more than 90% of what humans alone do in the current economic sphere. In that world, no AI and no robot can ever do the one thing we must do ourselves: Be a democracy. That exception creates the opportunity, but not the obligation, to be a full-time job for every citizen.

And as such, it deserves to be compensated in the same way Attic Greeks did over two millennia ago: through *misthos*.
